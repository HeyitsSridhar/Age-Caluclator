Interesting Facts About Age

Leap Year Babies

People born on February 29 are sometimes called "leaplings." There are about 5 million worldwide. They celebrate their actual birthday only once every four years.

Average Human Lifespan

Global average life expectancy is about 73 years — roughly 26,660 days, or 640,000 hours. It varies by country and has been steadily rising.

The Oldest Person Ever

Jeanne Calment of France lived to 122 years and 164 days — approximately 44,724 days of documented life.

Heartbeats in a Lifetime

The average heart beats about 100,000 times per day — roughly 2.7 billion beats over an average lifetime.
